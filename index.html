About the Property

Where the Jungle Meets Home

Nestled within nearly 19,000 square feet of lush tropical grounds, Casa Arecas is a rare offering in Sayulita - a private compound that feels worlds away while being minutes from town and the beach.

The heart of the home is a spacious great room crowned by a soaring palapa roof, creating an airy, open-air living experience that seamlessly blends indoor and outdoor living. A full kitchen and bar area anchor the space, perfect for entertaining or quiet mornings with coffee overlooking the jungle canopy.

The primary bedroom features an ensuite bathroom and walk-in closet, while loft spaces throughout the property add versatile living areas. A workshop and garage provide practical space for projects and vehicles.

Outside, the grounds are the true showpiece - a private pool and dipping pool sit surrounded by mature landscaping, connected by illuminated pathways that wind through the property's botanical garden-like setting. Every corner reveals something new: towering palms, flowering tropical plants, and the soothing sounds of nature.

With no HOA fees and strong rental income potential, Casa Arecas is a turn-key property ready for its next chapter - whether as a private residence, a vacation retreat, or an income-generating asset.

Location

Life in Sayulita

Sayulita is a vibrant surf town on Mexico's Pacific coast in the state of Nayarit, about 40 minutes north of Puerto Vallarta. Known for its laid-back atmosphere, world-class surf breaks, and thriving arts scene, it has become one of Mexico's most sought-after coastal communities.

From Casa Arecas, you're just minutes from the beach, local restaurants, boutique shops, and the lively town plaza. The surrounding Sierra Madre jungle provides endless opportunities for hiking, wildlife watching, and immersion in nature.

Beaches World-class surf & swimming beaches minutes away
Dining Farm-to-table restaurants, seafood, and vibrant food scene
Access 40 min from Puerto Vallarta International Airport (PVR)
